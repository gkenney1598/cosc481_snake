
# The Very Hungry Snake

A game heavily inspired by the classic Snake created using Raylib. Play as a snake, following the classic game rules, but eat 6 different types of fruit with varying difficulty and power-ups.
<img width="624" height="638" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/593447f0-f9e3-4fc5-97db-c5a6a230dc75" />





## Features

- 6 Different Fruits to eat, each animated on the screen. Lemon will move around the board using randomly generated directions, Watermelon will appear twice as big, Orange will speed up the snake, Pear will give you 2x your current score, Strawberry will half your score, and Apple will reset your speed back to normal.
  <img width="787" height="627" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/2e129f07-c2dc-454b-80d5-2224b2ef0599" />
- The Very Hungry Snake will speed up slowly as the game progresses, until you get lucky and eat an apple.
- Background music and SFX when a fruit is eaten.
- Press "P" to pause and unpause gameplay
- Game tracks your high score in each session


### Dev Mode

- Press "D" to enter dev mode, where the speed can be controlled through left and right brackets
- Pressing numbers will change the sprite being generated to one specific kind. Only the sprite is changed until it is eaten, then the rest of the fruit will have the corresponding feature.
- Hit boxes for the head of the snake and fruit will be displayed, as well as snake speed
- When in dev mode, eating Apple does not reset speed back to normal so that edited speed can continue to be used.
